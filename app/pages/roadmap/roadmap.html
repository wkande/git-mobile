Roadmap

We have a long list of functionality to roll into our release schedule. If you would like to comment or make a suggestion head over to GitHub wyomingsoftware/git-mobile-for-github.

  • v1.0.0 - 7/29/2016
  • v2.0.0 (drafting)

  • v3.0.0 (drafting)
